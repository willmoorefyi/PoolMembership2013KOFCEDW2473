Your pool application has been approved for the 2014 season.  Attached you will find our rules and policies for pool usage.  By applying for the 2014 pool membership you are agreeing to follow the rules and policies as stipulated in the provided document.  As a non-fraternal member of the Knights of Columbus- Edward Douglass White Council, your pool membership entitles you to the following:

Full use of the swimming pools
Full use of the Deep End Snack Bar
Full use of the picnic grounds
Full use of the Playground, volleyball/basketball/tennis courts

Please remember that the Council Home and Bar are exclusively for Knights of Columbus – Edward Douglass White Council members and their families and guests.

In addition, you will find a map attached indicating the appropriate places to park while on our property.  All pool members should park in the lower parking lot, indicated on the map by Site “C”.  Parking spaces surrounding the Council Home are reserved solely for Knights of Columbus- Edward Douglass White Council members actively using the Council Home.  We appreciate your cooperation in abiding by these parking regulations.
